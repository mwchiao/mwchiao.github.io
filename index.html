About Me

As the URL says, my name is Maxim Chiao. I am currently a 4th year, Computer Science and American Studies double major at the University of California, Davis. My expected graduation date is December, 2020.

I like programming and like to explore the various fields in the realm of Computer Science. I really like knowing a little bit of everything (in general) because you will never know when it will become a conversation starter or ice breaker one day.
I love looking at the nitty-gritty of how things work, it is interesting to me to see how things are broken down and how those pieces interact with each other. I also have a particular interest in lower-level languages like C/C++ because of how close they are to machine level and what that means for performance and things of that nature. (Don't worry higher level languages, I like you too!).

Besides Computer Science, my next favorite subject is history. I just like learning about the past and how civilizations have come and go, and how they contributed to the world today. I especially like history stuff that you have to look for and it not really mentioned in any history books.
